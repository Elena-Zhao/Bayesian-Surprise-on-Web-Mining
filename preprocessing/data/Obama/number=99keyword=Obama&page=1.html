How to Email Barack Obama

By eHow Contributor
  • Share
  • Pin
  • Tweet
  • Stumble
  • Share
Found This Helpful

So you want to email Barack Obama? It's easy. President Obama is breaking ground as one of the first presidential candidates to harness the power of the internet to reach people. He and his administration have said that they are committed to making the president more accessible than any other in history. If you want to email Obama, all you have to do is get on the internet. You can contact him through his website or through many of the social networking sites.

Things You'll Need

  • a computer
  • internet access

Instructions

    • 1

      EMAIL BARACK OBAMA. You can email President Barack Obama through the White House web page. Click the following link and then fill out the form: http://www.whitehouse.gov/contact/

    • 2

      SEND A MESSAGE. Another way to email Obama is through any of the social networking sites he belongs to. You can email Obama through Facebook, MySpace, YouTube, Flickr, Twitter, Linked In, BlackPlanet, FaithBase, Eons, and other social networking sites. For a complete list of sites that will let you leave a message for Obama, visit http://www.barackobama.com/index.php and scroll down to the "Obama Everywhere" sidebar on the bottom right.

    • 3

      SNAIL MAIL. If you would prefer to write a hand-written letter to President Obama, you can send him some snail mail to the following address: The White House
      1600 Pennsylvania Avenue NW
      Washington, DC 20500

    • 4

      PHONE. Another way to talk to Barack Obama is by leaving him a voice mail. You can call the White House and leave a comment on the following line: Comments: 202-456-1111
