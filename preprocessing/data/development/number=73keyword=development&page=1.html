Office of University Development and Alumni Relations

MISSION:

The Eastern Kentucky University Office of Development and Alumni Relations cultivates relationships and seeks philanthropic support through meaningful engagement

VISION:

The Eastern Kentucky University Office of Development and Alumni Relations will be a pivotal force in the EKU community by inspiring exemplary philanthropic support.

 CORE VALUES: 
  • Integrity – Do what is moral and ethical
  • Accountability – Maintain accuracy and transparency
  • Service – Actively respond to the needs of our constituents
  • Excellence – Strive for excellence in all we do
  • Tradition – Honor tradition; embrace innovation
