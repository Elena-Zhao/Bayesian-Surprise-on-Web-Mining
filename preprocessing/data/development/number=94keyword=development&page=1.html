SCAA Professional Development programs are based on industry-recognized standards and best practices, taught by carefully vetted, experienced instructors. Many classes can be applied toward professional certificates or credentials, which provide international recognition along with personal accomplishment.

Earning a professional certificate from SCAA is a clear indication of industry-accepted knowledge and skills with unparalleled credibility.

As the world's largest trade association for specialty coffee, SCAA classes, professional Certificates, and credentials provide access to individual professional development and provide value to companies throughout the specialty coffee supply chain.
