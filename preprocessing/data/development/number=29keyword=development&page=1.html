Development
Welcome to the Firebird Development Lab!

This site area is devoted to Firebird development efforts, and you are at the right place if you are interested to learn more about our current developments, future plans or organization structure.

The Firebird development efforts are distributed among a number of sub-projects:
  • Firebird Server
  • Quality Assurance
  • Documentation
  • JDBC Driver
  • ODBC Driver
  • .NET Provider
  • Python Driver
Please visit the particular sub-project area for the detailed information about its development.

The overall development activity of the project can be tracked online by following the checkins archive at Nabble.
