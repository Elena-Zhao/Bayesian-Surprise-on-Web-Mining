FAQs

Q: Do I need a visa to study English in the UK?

A: Yes, unless you are a national of a country in the European Economic Area or Switzerland.

Q: Can we get any advice on passing our accreditation inspection?

A: Yes: we know it can be daunting so we offer an Accreditation Consultancy Service to help you through the process.
