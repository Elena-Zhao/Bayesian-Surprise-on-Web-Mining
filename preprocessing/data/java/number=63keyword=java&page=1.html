Java Java

Our atmosphere is very cozy and comfortable. We like to think of ourselves as a neighborhood favorite while popular with the entire City of Houston. Please print and use our COUPON found on the front page of this web site and under Internet Specials.

In 1990 the building now housing Java Java Cafe was HEIGHTS ABOVE FLORIST. Many of you may remember it! Matt Parsons and partners come along in 1993 and opened a coffee and dessert shop in the building, hence the name Java Java. They still sold flowers at that time.

Soon after they started serving breakfast, lunch and dinner and they were open 24 hours a day. Some time later Matt bought out his partners to operate Java Jave Cafe on his own. That has continued until today. More recently Matt decided to concentrate on serving wonderful breakfasts and lunches.

OUR CHEF HAS BEEN WITH JAVA JAVA CAFE FOR 18 YEARS! WE HAVE FRESH FOOD COOKED TO ORDER.

We are known for our chicken Salad that can be stuffed in an avocado or served tropical with pinapple, our cornbread waffles, creamy cheese grits and homemade Key Lime Pie. Please come on in and join us for your next breakfast, lunch or weekend brunch.
