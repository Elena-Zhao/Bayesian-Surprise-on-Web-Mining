Most Valuable Person Designation

 MOST VALUABLE PERSON

Employees nominated for City M.V.P. status are evaluated for their overall interaction with other employees, their reliability, their consistency, and their job performance. 

Award recipients are selected by the M.V.P. Team, comprised of prior M.V.P. designees.
