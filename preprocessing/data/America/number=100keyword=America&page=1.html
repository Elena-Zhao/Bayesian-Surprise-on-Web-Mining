Since 1989 America Today translates and creates the all American campus lifestyle in a wide range of very relaxed basics, accessories and college classics like jeans, T-shirts, sweaters, sweatpants and hoodies with distinctive logos, prints and details. America Today focuses on students: entrepreneurial, active, open-minded guys and girls who love casual jeans and chinos, tees, longsleeves and hooded sweats.

We make clothing that lets you easily finish your busy schedule: racing from college to a library, from the coffee shop to a date, from your second job to the gym and to end the day in your favorite café. You don’t have to worry about your outfit, we, at America Today, did that for you! What is most important, is that you feel comfortable in America Today clothes and that you will look fantastic anytime, anyplace, anywhere.
