America Ferrera

Actress | Soundtrack | Producer
America Ferrera was born on April 18, 1984 in Los Angeles, California, USA as America Georgine Ferrera. She is an actress, known for Ugly Betty (2006), Hikku to doragon (2010) and How to Train Your Dragon 2 (2014). She has been married to Ryan Piers Williams since June 27, 2011. See full bio »
