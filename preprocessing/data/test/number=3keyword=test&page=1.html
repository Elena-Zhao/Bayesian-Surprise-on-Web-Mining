Two Cautions

  1. If English (American) is not your native language, then your reported IQ score will probably be lower than it would be if you took an IQ test in your own language.
  2. If you feel you may have an educational problem, a learning disability, or a psychological problem, then you should consult with a professional psychologist or psychiatrist before you take this test. It is quite possible to have superior intelligence and yet still have difficulties that may require professional help. Scoring well on any IQ test is no guarantee of success in life. Professional psychological help can sometimes make all the difference between partially or fully realizing your potential.
